Pizza
Pizza is a universally loved dish featuring a crispy base layered with rich tomato sauce, gooey melted cheese, and an array of colorful vegetable toppings. This versatile dish can be tailored to suit any taste, from classic Margherita to vibrant veggie-loaded creations. Whether baked in a traditional oven or made at home, pizza brings joy to gatherings and mealtimes alike.
Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Total Time: 35 min
0 Likes

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza base
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup toppings (bell peppers, onions, mushrooms, olives)
  • Italian seasoning and chili flakes

Preparation

  1. Spread pizza sauce evenly over the base.
  2. Add toppings and sprinkle cheese generously.
  3. Bake at 200°C (400°F) for 15-20 minutes or until cheese is bubbly.
