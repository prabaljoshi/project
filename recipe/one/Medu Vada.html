Medu Vada
Medu vada is a traditional South Indian snack thatâ€™s loved for its crispy exterior and soft, fluffy interior. Made from a spiced urad dal batter and deep-fried to golden perfection, these doughnut-shaped fritters are a delightful combination of flavor and texture. Often served with coconut chutney and sambar, medu vada is a favorite breakfast or tea-time treat.
Prep Time: 10 min
Cook Time: 20 min
Total Time: 30 min
0 Likes

Ingredients

  • 1 cup urad dal
  • 1 green chili (finely chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger (grated)
  • Curry leaves and coriander leaves (finely chopped)
  • Salt and oil for frying

Preparation

  1. Soak urad dal for 4-5 hours and grind into a smooth, fluffy batter.
  2. Mix in spices, herbs, and salt. Shape into small doughnuts and deep-fry until golden brown.
  3. Serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.
